Is This Weird?

Musculoskeletal conditions affect more than 1.7 billion people world wide. These conditions are the most common cause of severe, long-term pain and disability. They are responsible for 30 million emergency department visits annually and increasing.

Chances are if you go to an emergency room complaing of limb pain, you are going to have an x-ray done. This abnormality detection app uses deep learning to assist doctors in reading x-rays in order to expidite clinical decisions. The app utilizes a convolutional neural network trained on 40,561 upper limb images labeled by board-certified radiologists from the Stanford Hospital.

Usage Possibilities

Help practitioners identify possible abnormalities in an x-ray before having a radiologists reading
Help practitioners in remote areas with x-ray interpretation where access to skilled radiologists is limited
